Our Services

✽

We seek to being a highly professional and socially responsible organization with reputation to completing customer satisfaction and creating values for stakeholders. We extend delivery of our services nationwide.

Poultry farming

We breed chickens, ducks, turkeys and geese for their meat and eggs. Our chickens are availble in cockerels, layers and broilers while the turkeys are in local and agric.

Fish farming

We breed fresh fish in our tanks and ponds and also prepare smoked fish in all range of sizes for food.

Snail farming

We breed land snails in a large-scale in all range of sizes for their meat.

Cattle farming

We will be commercially breeding cattle for their meat, milk and skin soon. Our produce is still on a low-scale and our service is thus not fully available.

Goat, Ram & Sheep farming

We will be commercially breeding goats, rams and sheep for their meat, milk, skin and fibre soon. Our produce is still on a low-scale and our service is thus not fully available.

Livestock feeds

We produce processed and raw feeds for livestocks in poultry, fish and snail farming. These feeds are packed in different sizes for different stages of the livestocks.

Farm owner training

We train people to becoming farm owners of their own. They are trained through management of the farmland as well as care of livestocks and production of feeds.

Who we are

✽
Larry Global Farm & Agro Services is a developing company in large poultry production, commercial livestocks production, feedmilling, aquaculture and technical training service. Established in 2010, we have grown over the years in service of production and distribution nationwide. We are also involved in extension of service to educate and inform prospective farmers on the most viable husbandary practices. Our vision is to be the foremost operator in the Nigerian livestock industry, in which will be able to sell more than 50% of our products outside the country. We seek to being a highly professional and socially responsible organization with reputation to completing customer satisfaction and creating values for stakeholders. We continue to maintain quality, trust, integrity, teamwork and professionalism.
